Information Systems Analysis and Design

Having a professor who is a working professional and interacts with systems every day has made this class truly rewarding. Learning about the process of creating and implementing an information system has been an invaluable skill to add to my tool box.

This course is extremely enjoyable for me becuase I can directly relate many of the topics to my internship experience over the summer. Making and Manipulating Race

This course has raised my awareness about the historical ways in which public perceptions of race and identity have been formed. This has been a very interesting course that has shown me how particular tropes concerning African Americans were circulated, ultimately to the detriment of this racial group.
